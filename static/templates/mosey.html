Mosey is a week of fun events, usually during the first couple weeks of Spring semester, where students who are interested in living in a co-op get to tour the houses, then get to know the members of the houses they are most interested in living in.

Mosey 2014!

If you aren't able to make it to the open house on Sunday, don't worry! Contact us to get on our mosey mailing list, or check back later for the online form. Hope to see you soon!
